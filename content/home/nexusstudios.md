## Overview

Developed a pipeline for [Nexus Studios](https://nexusstudios.com/) to transfer volumetric assets from Houdini into Unity's VFX Graph for real-time rendering. Created video tutorials for their tech art team.

## Houdini to Unity Pipeline

Built tooling to export volumetric simulation data from Houdini and import it into Unity's VFX Graph system, enabling real-time playback of complex volumetric effects that were previously offline-only.
